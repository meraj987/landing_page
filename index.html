One Roof Community Centre

One Roof Community Centre is open 365 days a year and serves free meals for our community every day. One Roof offers life skills development, community building, harm reduction, access to primary health care and a safe space to be. Friendships are made, achievements are celebrated and hardships are overcome. We are located at 99 Brock St.
